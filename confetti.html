ABOUT US
Confetti Lounge was created by a Mother-in-law/ Daughter-in-law dynamic duo. This fun loving pair designed Confetti Lounge so that little ones can have a special place to play during any type of celebration. Our play lounges promote a safe place where children can play, learn, and laugh. We showcase a modern and clean aesthetic that can be easily paired with most party themes. Our Confetti Crew team members take the time with each client to guide you through the booking process, and we can customize each play lounge to fit your party's needs.
